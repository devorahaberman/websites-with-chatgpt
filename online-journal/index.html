Bork & Banter

One giant dog, one human, endless adventures.

Chunk's New Favorite Nap Spot

Dec 3, 2025

Today Chunk discovered that the laundry basket (filled, of course) is officially a luxury bed. He settled in, hogged the blanket, and performed the patented Mastiff snore-symphony while I attempted to fold two shirts. The zoomies came at sunset and the couch lost dignity. 10/10 would recommend investing in a bigger basket.

The Great Drool Pool Incident

Dec 2, 2025

Took Chunk to the park and he found a puddle that was basically a small ocean. He treated it like a spa, splashing, rolling, and generously redecorating my shoes. Spent the evening with towels, treats, and a surprisingly calm Chunk who clearly felt cleansed by his muddy dip.

Mailman vs. The Gentle Giant

Dec 1, 2025

A very important package arrived. Chunk announced its arrival with a single, dignified bork — then inspected the box for quality control. The mail carrier gave Chunk a biscuit and then treated him like royalty. Both parties left satisfied.

First Snow: Observations From the Porch

Nov 30, 2025

The first dusting of snow made Chunk act like he'd never seen flakes before — pensive, then suspicious, then ecstatic. He attempted to catch a snowflake and ended up with a frosty beard. Spent 20 minutes trying to convince him that snow is not edible (he disagrees).
